About us

Founded in 1995 by Sarah Crane, Someliers has grown from a one-woman endeavor into a thriving team of six passionate individuals. Sarah's journey began with a vision to bring a touch of excellence to every event through the art of wine selection and pairing. What started as a solo venture has now blossomed into a dedicated team of experts, all sharing a common goal: to make your events truly extraordinary. Our team understands that it's not just about food and wine; it's about people, connections, and creating moments that last a lifetime.

Our commitment goes beyond just pairing wine with food; we tailor each selection to match the individual and their specific needs. We believe that it's these personal touches that illuminate any event, adding a touch of brilliance and making it truly memorable. At Someliers, we make your events shine by turning wine into an art that connects people and transforms ordinary moments into extraordinary memories.
